# ServifyEU Refund Policy

## When Are Refunds Issued?

Refunds are issued in the following circumstances:

1. **Booking cancelled by customer** — see Cancellation Policy for amounts
2. **Booking cancelled by provider** — full refund + €10/£10 credit
3. **Service not completed** — full refund after investigation
4. **Service quality dispute** — partial or full refund at ServifyEU's discretion after investigation
5. **Payment processing error** — full refund of duplicate charges

## How Long Do Refunds Take?

- **Credit/debit cards**: 5–10 business days
- **SEPA Direct Debit**: up to 14 business days
- **iDEAL**: 3–5 business days
- **Klarna**: 5–7 business days

## Platform Credits

Platform credits are:
- Valid for 12 months from issue date
- Cannot be exchanged for cash
- Cannot be transferred to another account
- Applied automatically to your next booking

## Disputed Refunds

If you disagree with a refund amount:
1. Contact support with your booking number
2. Explain the issue
3. Our team reviews within 2 business days
4. Claims over €200 require management approval

## VAT Refunds

VAT is always refunded proportionally to the refund amount. A VAT credit note is issued for all refunds.
