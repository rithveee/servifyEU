# ServifyEU Cancellation Policy

## Customer Cancellations

Customers may cancel a booking at any time before the scheduled service. The refund amount depends on how far in advance the cancellation is made:

- **More than 48 hours before**: Full refund (100%)
- **24–48 hours before**: 50% refund
- **Less than 24 hours before**: No refund

## Provider Cancellations

If a provider cancels a booking, the customer receives:
- A full refund (100%)
- A €10 / £10 platform credit as compensation for the inconvenience

## How to Cancel

1. Log into your ServifyEU account
2. Go to "My Bookings"
3. Click on the booking you wish to cancel
4. Click "Cancel Booking" and confirm

You can also contact our support team via chat, email, or phone.

## Refund Processing

Refunds are processed within 5–10 business days and returned to the original payment method. The time to appear in your account depends on your bank or card issuer.

For SEPA payments, refunds may take up to 14 business days.

## No-Show Policy

If a customer is not present when the provider arrives, and the provider cannot complete the job, this is treated as a late cancellation (less than 24 hours). No refund applies.

If a provider does not show up, customers receive a full refund and a platform credit.

## Disputes

If you believe a cancellation was handled incorrectly, please contact our support team with your booking reference number. We will review within 2 business days.
