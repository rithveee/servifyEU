# ServifyEU FAQ — General

## What is ServifyEU?

ServifyEU is Europe's home services marketplace. We connect you with vetted, background-checked service professionals for cleaning, plumbing, electrical work, beauty treatments, and more — all bookable online in minutes.

## Which countries do you operate in?

We currently operate in:
- United Kingdom (GBP)
- Germany (EUR)
- France (EUR)
- Netherlands (EUR)
- Spain (EUR)

## How do I book a service?

1. Search for the service you need on our website or app
2. Select your preferred date and time
3. Choose a provider (or let us auto-assign the best match)
4. Enter your address and payment details
5. Confirm your booking — you'll receive a confirmation email instantly

## Are your providers vetted?

Yes. All ServifyEU providers go through:
- ID verification
- Background check (where applicable by country)
- KYC (Know Your Customer) document review
- Skills verification

## How are prices calculated?

Prices are based on the service type, duration, and your location. VAT is included in the final price shown at checkout. Providers may set custom prices for their services.

## What payment methods do you accept?

- Credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- SEPA Direct Debit (Germany, France, Netherlands, Spain)
- iDEAL (Netherlands)
- Klarna (buy now, pay later)

## Is my payment secure?

Yes. All payments are processed by Stripe, a PCI DSS Level 1 certified payment provider. We never store your card details.

## Can I reschedule a booking?

Yes, as long as you reschedule more than 24 hours before the scheduled time. Go to "My Bookings" and click "Reschedule".

## What if I'm not happy with the service?

Contact our support team within 24 hours of the service. We'll investigate and, if appropriate, arrange a re-service or issue a refund.

## How do I contact support?

- Live chat: available on our website (bottom-right chat button)
- Email: support@servifyeu.com
- Phone: See our Contact page for country-specific numbers

## Do you offer GDPR data export?

Yes. Under GDPR, you have the right to request all your personal data. Log in, go to Account Settings > Privacy, and click "Export My Data".
